\section{Research interests}

\cvline{}{Much of my current research is about higher-dimensional (4D and higher) data models, data structures and algorithms for Geographic Information Systems, using these dimensions to model not only space, but also other characteristics such as time and scale. I am also interested in other topics that combine geometric/topological computing and spatial information, such as geometric modelling, the validation and repair of geographic data, topological data structures, and visualising spatial data. I like theoretical work, but what I enjoy most is implementing my ideas into working prototypes and, when they are serious enough, into free software.}
